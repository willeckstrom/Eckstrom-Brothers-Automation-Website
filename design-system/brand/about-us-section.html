About EBA

Will Eckstrom

Co-Founder

Will learned construction in the field and warehouse, working across California Drywall's prefabrication operations, project engineering, and preconstruction. Now bridging family heritage with modern automation, Will leads EBA's mission to eliminate manual administrative work that slows down GCs and subs. His hands-on understanding of jobsite realities ensures EBA's automation solves problems that matter.

Steve Eckstrom

Co-Founder

CEO of California Drywall, Steve is a living legend in North American construction. Over decades, he transformed the company from a single-trade drywall firm into a diversified specialty contractor spanning metal framing, acoustical ceilings, fireproofing, and paint. His pioneering vision for prefabrication and off-site manufacturing has reshaped how California Drywall executes work. A third-generation leader who started as a laborer, Steve brings unmatched field knowledge and relentless drive for innovation to EBA.
